# Moonlit Match Agent Instructions

## Project

Moonlit Match is a Pinterest-driven astrology and soulmate guidance brand.

The current business goal is fastest compliant affiliate sales possible first, while building a long-term brand asset.

## Current Funnel

Pinterest Pin → Moonlit Match bridge page → ClickBank affiliate offer.

Do not create or recommend direct Pinterest-to-affiliate linking.

Primary bridge pages:

- `/moon-reading/`
- `/soulmate-sketch/`

## Hard Compliance Rules

1. Pinterest pins must link to Moonlit Match bridge pages first.
2. Do not direct-link Pinterest pins to ClickBank affiliate offers.
3. Pins linking to affiliate bridge pages must include an affiliate disclosure.
4. Place `#affiliate` or `#ad` discreetly at the very end of the pin description.
5. Prefer placing the disclosure a couple of line breaks after the last normal description text and after all other hashtags.
6. Use compliance-safe language:
   - "may reveal"
   - "explore"
   - "symbolic insight"
   - "self-reflection"
   - "patterns"
   - "clarity"
7. Avoid certainty or guaranteed-result claims:
   - Do not say "you will meet your soulmate."
   - Do not say "this guarantees love."
   - Do not say "your reading proves..."
8. Include affiliate disclosure text on bridge pages.

## Development Rules

1. Do not convert the project to TypeScript.
2. Do not add a `src/` directory unless explicitly requested.
3. Do not install new packages unless explicitly approved.
4. Do not run `npm audit fix --force`.
5. Keep pages fast, responsive, and SEO-friendly.
6. Prefer reusable components when a pattern appears more than once.
7. Keep copy premium, emotionally intelligent, and compliance-safe.
8. Preserve the Moonlit Match brand name.
9. Test locally before recommending commit/push.
10. After each milestone, commit and push.
